TradeGuard Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 25 June 2026

TradeGuard respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Encrypted Password
  • Subscription Information
  • MT5 Account Number (if provided)
  • Broker Information (if provided)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your TradeGuard account.
  • Provide access to TradeGuard EA services.
  • Manage your subscription and free trial.
  • Send important account notifications.
  • Respond to customer support requests.
  • Improve our products and services.

3. Password Security

Passwords are encrypted using industry-standard hashing techniques and are never stored in plain text. TradeGuard administrators cannot view your password.

4. Data Protection

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Sharing Your Information

TradeGuard does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Information may only be shared when required by law or to provide the services you have requested.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

TradeGuard may use browser cookies or local storage to keep you signed in, remember your preferences, and improve your experience.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your personal information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account, subject to legal or contractual obligations.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, please contact the TradeGuard support team.
