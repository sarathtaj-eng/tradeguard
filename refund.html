TradeGuard Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

This Refund Policy explains how subscription payments, cancellations, and refunds are handled for TradeGuard products and services.

1. Subscription Plans

TradeGuard subscriptions are billed according to the pricing plan selected by the user at the time of purchase.

2. Free Trial

Where available, users are encouraged to evaluate TradeGuard using the free trial period before purchasing a paid subscription.

3. Refund Policy

Once a subscription has been activated, payments are generally non-refundable. Please review the selected plan carefully before completing your purchase.

4. Technical Issues

If TradeGuard fails to function due to a verified software defect, our support team will investigate the issue and provide assistance. Refund requests arising from verified software problems will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

5. Cancellation

Users may cancel future subscription renewals at any time through their TradeGuard dashboard. Cancellation prevents future billing but does not automatically refund previous payments.

6. Contact Support

For refund requests or billing inquiries, please contact our support team:

Email: support@tradeguard.com
